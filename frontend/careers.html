Careers at Green Pacific

Join our team and help keep Vancouver spotless

Become Part of Our Crew

We’re always looking for hardworking, detail-oriented people to join our Green Pacific family. Working with us means more than just cleaning—it’s about building relationships, learning continuously and taking pride in making spaces shine.

1. Registration

Submit your application by clicking the button below. We’ll review your details and let you know next steps.

2. Document Submission & Requirements

Successful applicants will be asked to provide government‑issued ID, a recent photo and a police background check. These documents ensure the safety and trust of our clients and crews.

3. Video Interview

We conduct interviews via video call using a secure messaging app. This allows us to validate your identity and answer any questions you may have.

4. Background & Reference Checks

References and documentation are verified to ensure that all crew members meet our high standards.

5. WHMIS Training

You’ll need to complete Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System training. We provide a free training video to help you achieve certification.

6. Ready for Work

Once all steps are complete, you’ll be ready to join our crews and start working on-site.

7. Insurance

Protect yourself with liability insurance through our partner program. Affordable plans are available and certificates are issued immediately.

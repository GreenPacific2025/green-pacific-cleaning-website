Our Story

Founded in Vancouver, Green Pacific Cleaning began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, reliable cleaning services that empower local crews and support our community. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for businesses across the Lower Mainland.

Our Mission & Values

We believe in delivering exceptional cleaning services while upholding values of integrity, accountability and environmental responsibility. Our A+ accreditation reflects our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Team

Our crews are the backbone of our success. We invest in training, fair compensation and a positive work environment to ensure every team member takes pride in their work and in serving our clients.

Environment & Community

We’re committed to environmentally friendly practices and giving back to the communities we serve. From using eco-friendly products to supporting local initiatives, we strive to make a positive impact.

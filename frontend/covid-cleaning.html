Enhanced Cleaning Procedures

At Green Pacific, the health and safety of your employees and clients is our top priority. Our crews follow industry‑standard guidelines for cleaning and disinfection, with additional enhancements to reduce the spread of viruses and pathogens.

  • Increased frequency of disinfecting high‑touch surfaces such as door handles, light switches and washroom fixtures.
  • Use of appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) by all cleaning staff during service.
  • Optional electrostatic disinfection treatments and fogging on request.
  • Collaboration with your management team to implement any site‑specific protocols you may require.

If your facility has special requirements—such as physical distancing guidelines or occupancy restrictions—we are happy to incorporate them into our service plan. Our account managers coordinate with you to schedule extended services or after‑hours cleaning to minimize disruption.

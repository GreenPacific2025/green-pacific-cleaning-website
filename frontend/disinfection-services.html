Four Levels of Disinfection

From routine wipe‑downs to intensive sanitation, Green Pacific offers a range of disinfection services designed to meet the needs of your facility. Our specialists will work with you to determine the appropriate level of cleaning and disinfection.

  • Facility Pre‑Clean: Remove dirt, dust and debris to eliminate cross‑contamination before disinfection.
  • Disinfection & Sanitization: Regular service using Health Canada‑approved disinfectants to reduce germs on high‑touch surfaces.
  • Detailed Disinfection: Targeted attention to frequently used items and high‑traffic areas, applying advanced disinfecting techniques.
  • Deep Disinfection: A thorough, top‑to‑bottom clean followed by specialized disinfection procedures for maximum protection.

No matter which level you choose, our teams follow strict protocols and use appropriate PPE to protect your people and our crew members.

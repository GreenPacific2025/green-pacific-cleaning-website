Common Questions

Will the same cleaning crew member be assigned to my location each time?

Absolutely. We strive to build trust and consistency by assigning the crew member most familiar with your facility. Over time, our crews become an extension of your team, giving you confidence in the quality and reliability of our service.

What determines the cost of professional cleaning services?

Pricing is primarily based on travel time to your facility and the time required on-site. That’s why choosing a local cleaning crew helps keep costs reasonable. We also offer flexible scheduling and can tailor a custom cleaning plan to fit your budget.

What does a commercial cleaning visit entail?

Each visit includes dusting, sweeping, mopping, disinfecting washrooms, removing trash and thoroughly cleaning visible surfaces. We also offer add‑ons such as carpet cleaning, floor care, high dusting, window cleaning and power washing to keep your space fresh.

How do you ensure consistent quality?

We regularly perform quality control visits and encourage client feedback. You can call us any time, and our mobile app makes it easy to submit service requests or report issues. We’re committed to continuous improvement and delivering consistent excellence.

How do I get a background check or WHMIS certification?

For prospective crew members, we provide detailed instructions during the hiring process. Background checks and WHMIS training are essential requirements to ensure safety and compliance. We can guide you through obtaining the necessary documentation.

How soon can I expect a response to my inquiry?

Our team monitors inquiries 24/7. We strive to respond as quickly as possible—typically within one business day. For urgent matters, please call us at (604) 440-3569.

Still have questions?

Contact us for more information or to discuss your specific needs. We’re here to help.

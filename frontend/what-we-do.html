Trusted Partner for Your Business

At Green Pacific Cleaning, we believe that a clean and hygienic environment is central to the success of any business. Our goal is to become your trusted partner for life, delivering reliable commercial and facility cleaning services that meet the unique needs of every client we serve. From single offices to large commercial complexes, we work closely with you to understand your operations, schedule and budget—building a program that keeps your spaces spotless and your workforce healthy.

Our professional cleaning crews are fully trained, insured and equipped with the latest technologies to deliver consistent results. Whether you require routine office cleaning, specialized floor care, deep disinfection or one‑off event cleanup, we’re here to help. We proudly serve Vancouver and the Lower Mainland with flexible packages, no long‑term contracts, 24/7 customer support and a commitment to environmental responsibility.

Our Core Services

  • Office Cleaning: Keeping workspaces spotless, hygienic and productive.
  • Commercial Cleaning: Tailored solutions for retail, restaurants and commercial facilities.
  • Specialty Services: From dry ice blasting to window cleaning, we handle unique challenges.
  • Service Areas: Dedicated local crews serving Vancouver, Surrey, Burnaby, Richmond and beyond.

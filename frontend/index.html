Our Employee Sense

At Green Pacific Cleaning, we believe in empowering our team members and helping them thrive in delivering trusted, high-quality commercial cleaning services.

We focus on building a strong sense of ownership, encouraging collaboration, and enhancing our employees’ problem-solving skills. Our crews work with professionalism and pride, guided by values of accountability, self-reflection, and respect — the foundations of being a Green Pacific team member.

Our goal is to cultivate high-performing cleaning professionals who are determined, capable, and passionate about providing the exceptional service and reliability our clients across Vancouver have come to expect.

The Human Touch in Cleaning

At Green Pacific Cleaning, we don’t just clean—we connect. We prioritize the human touch in everything we do, forging genuine relationships between our clients, cleaning crews and company leadership. The best service comes from understanding the people we serve, and we take the time to know your business and your unique needs.

Our people-centric, systems-based approach ensures the consistency and quality you expect while retaining the benefits of working with local, independent crews. We invest in our team members through continuous training and fair compensation to foster accountability, collaboration and pride in every space we clean. We’re committed to environmentally responsible practices and caring for the facilities we service—reflecting our dedication to both people and planet.

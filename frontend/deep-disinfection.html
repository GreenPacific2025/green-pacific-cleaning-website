When You Need the Deepest Clean

Deep disinfection combines intensive cleaning with specialized disinfection techniques. We start by thoroughly cleaning your facility—removing dust, debris and contaminants that can prevent disinfectants from working effectively. Once the surfaces are clean, we apply hospital‑grade disinfectants using methods such as electrostatic spraying to ensure full coverage.

This service is ideal for facilities with high infection risk or after an outbreak has occurred. We recommend including a pre‑clean as part of the process to minimize cross‑contamination.

Why Pre‑Clean?

Cleaning and disinfection go hand in hand. A facility pre‑clean removes dirt, dust and debris that can harbour viruses and bacteria, reducing the risk of cross‑contamination and making subsequent disinfection more effective.

Our pre‑clean service includes thorough vacuuming, sweeping and wiping of all surfaces to eliminate foreign contaminants. This step is recommended prior to any detailed or deep disinfection to ensure disinfectants can work efficiently.
